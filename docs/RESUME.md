## Long (portfolio paragraph)

Developed **Telugu NewsAI**, an AI-powered content automation platform for Telugu news organizations. The system accepts long-form Telugu articles and produces Facebook posts, Instagram captions, Twitter/X posts, WhatsApp share text, YouTube community posts, breaking news alerts, hashtags, SEO keywords, headline extraction, key facts, and three caption variations—while enforcing factual accuracy through prompt engineering. The stack includes a **Next.js 14** frontend with React Query, an **Express.js** backend with **PostgreSQL** and **Prisma**, **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** for generation, and **JWT**-based authentication. Features include a real-time analytics dashboard, searchable generation history, rate limiting, and deployment documentation for **Vercel** and **Railway**.
